Grilled Cheese

Description

This sandwich is made following the recipe that my great-Grandfather wrote down when he used to make it for my dad. It is a traditional New England-style grilled cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Cheese.
  • 1 cup Grilled.
  • A whole lot of love.

Steps

  1. Heat a large pot on the stove.
  2. Add 2 cups water and the cheese.
  3. Once boiling, add grill and other yummy stuff.
  4. Let simmer for 3 hours and enjoy!
