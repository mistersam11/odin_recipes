Chowder

Description

This chowder is made following the recipe that my great-Grandmother wrote down when she used to make it for my mom. It is a traditional New England-style chowder.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Clams.
  • 1 cup Flower.
  • A whole lot of love.

Steps

  1. Heat a large pot on the stove.
  2. Add 2 cups water and the flower.
  3. Once boiling, add clams and other yummy stuff.
  4. Let simmer for 3 hours and enjoy!
