Steak

Description

This steak is made following the recipe that my great-Aunt wrote down when she used to make it for my cousin. It is a traditional New England-style steak.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound cow.
  • 1 cup butter.
  • A whole lot of love.

Steps

  1. Heat a large pot on the stove.
  2. Add 2 cups water and the steak.
  3. Once boiling, add butter and other yummy stuff.
  4. Let simmer for 3 hours and enjoy!
